Reachel's step up dance class

Rachel is an experienced teacher, choreographer and dancer with a professional history extending over 30 years. She prides herself in meticulous instruction of technique, passing on her love of dance to all students under her care, with great success in all levels of RAD examination and competitive work. Her wealth of knowledge comes from running her own studio, dancing with the Ontario Ballet Theatre and studying under many internationally known teachers, choreographers and principal dancers.
